The story of a frustrated shopper

  • I love shopping but problem is that after reaching home I realize that I forgot to buy some item or other.
  • Even if I remember to buy every item, once I reach home my wife realizes that she forgot to inform me to buy some item or other.
  • Sometimes while shopping I keep getting mutliple calls or messages from my wife asking me to buy 'this' or 'that' too. Its so irritating...
  • That's when I started using my note keeping app for shopping. It solved my problem a little. But still I do five 'pradakshin'(rounds) of the shopping mart before I pick all the items. Because this stupid notes app does not know how to group similar items.
  • Not to forget that the calls and messages from wife still continues. Now I have the extra task of copying from message to note app
  • I tried few apps, those ask me to login. You tell me why should I share my email or phone with some unknown app? Moreover my phone is full of useless apps. I dont want to install another app to drain battery.
  • Then I found QuickShoppingList. No installation, No login, just use it whenever I want it. Similar items are grouped so that I dont have to do multiple rounds in the shopping mall. Bindaas..

    You know what the best part is? No more messages/calls from wife :D. I share my shopping list with her and she can update the list while I am shopping. Finally an application which is as smart and simple as me. I am loving my shopping these days. Why don't you give a try?
